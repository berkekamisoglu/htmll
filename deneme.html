Who are we?

PXL University College is a young, dynamic and vibrant organisation, a centre of expertise for innovation, creativity and entrepreneurship.

PXL offers professional bachelor programmes in Business, Education, Healthcare, IT, Media & Tourism, Music, Social Work and Technology, and bachelor and master programmes in Arts.

PXL University College is located within walking distance of the commercial center of Hasselt.
